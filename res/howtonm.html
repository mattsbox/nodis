How to Use

Nodis.me is a location-based music search app that uses Apple Maps proprietary technology and nodis.me's own search engine optimization analytics to display song titles and information to the user allocating songs around their area. Users can easily determine what genre of music they'd like to explore by using our innovative 'Groove Compass' to highlight genres they'd like to hear and ultimately filter out unwanted genres for an efficient and accurate search experience. Nodis.me also displays trending music and their locations within the user's specified region.Our top-of-the-line team of designers and developers ensure a fast and easy way to discover new music. Join our movement by downloading the mobile app on your smartphones and tablets.
